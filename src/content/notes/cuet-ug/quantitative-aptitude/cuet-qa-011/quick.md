## Data Interpretation — Quick Reference

### Key Formula/Rule
Pie chart angle = (Component Value / Total Value) × 360° — this is the single most-used DI formula

### Memory Trick
**"Pie is 360° of a circle"** — each slice of a pie chart is a fraction of the whole 360° pizza. Percentage = (Angle / 360) × 100. For bar and line: just READ what's shown, no formula needed!

### 1-Sentence Summary
Data Interpretation tests how quickly and accurately you can read information from visual charts and tables.

### 30-Second Example
Q: In a pie chart where Rice production is 30% of total, what is the central angle for Rice?
A: 108° — (30/100) × 360 = 108
