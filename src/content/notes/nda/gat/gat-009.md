# Current Affairs

### 🟢 Lite — Quick Review (1h–1d)
> Rapid summary for last-minute revision before your exam.

**What are Current Affairs?**
Current affairs refer to recent events, news, and developments happening around the world and in India. For NDA exam, questions focus on national and international importance, government policies, awards, sports, and defence matters.

**Key Categories to Cover:**

1. **National News:** Government schemes, bills, elections, RBI announcements
2. **International News:** UN sessions, summits, treaties, global events
3. **Sports:** Major tournaments, medal winners, new records
4. **Awards and Honours:** Padma awards, Nobel, major literary prizes
5. **Defence News:** Exercises, new inductions, appointments in Forces

**Important Government Bodies for NDA:**
- President, Vice President, Prime Minister, Cabinet
- Parliament and its sessions
- Supreme Court judgments
- Election Commission

**Common Exam Focus Areas:**
- Union Budget (usually February)
- Monsoon Session of Parliament
- G20 summits and outcomes
- Quad, BRICS developments
- Indian Army/Navy/Air Force exercises

⚡ **NDA Tip:** Focus on Indian current affairs more than international. Recent government schemes and Indian achievements in defence and space are frequently asked.

⚡ **Common Mistake:** Don't try to memorise everything. Focus on understanding major events and their significance. Dates are important — "when" often matters.

**Worked Examples:**

*Example 1 (NDA Pattern):* In which year was the G20 presidency held by India?
Answer: 2023. India hosted the G20 Summit in New Delhi in September 2023.

*Example 2:* Which scheme aims to provide free electricity through rooftop solar?
PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana was launched in 2024 (or check for your exam year).

*Example 3:* Name the joint military exercise between India and Israel.
Burning Cloud or DESERT FLAG are exercises with different partners. India-Israel joint exercise is called "MIDDLE CLOUD"... Actually India and Israel do conduct joint exercises, typically called "DESERT KNIGHTS" or similar.

Actually India-Israel defence cooperation includes military equipment purchases and joint exercises called "Blue Flag" (with Israel hosting).
