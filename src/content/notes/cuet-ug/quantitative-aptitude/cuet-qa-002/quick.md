## Simplification & BODMAS — Quick Reference

### Key Formula/Rule
**BODMAS Order:** Brackets → Orders (powers/roots) → Division → Multiplication → Addition → Subtraction

### Memory Trick
"B**O**D**M**A**S**" — sing it to the tune of "Bodmas!" like a cheer. Or remember: **B**ig **O**pens **D**rive **M**y **A**uto **S**afely. The order is sacred — never skip steps!

### 1-Sentence Summary
BODMAS tells you exactly which operation to do first when an expression has multiple operations stacked together.

### 30-Second Example
Q: Simplify 3 + 6 × (5 − 2)
A: **21** — brackets first: 3 + 6 × 3, then multiplication: 3 + 18 = 21.
