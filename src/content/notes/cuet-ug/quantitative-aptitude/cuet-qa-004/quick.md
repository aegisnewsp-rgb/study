## Percentage & Profit-Loss — Quick Reference

### Key Formula/Rule
Percentage = (Part ÷ Whole) × 100. Profit = SP − CP. Loss = CP − SP. Gain% = (Gain ÷ CP) × 100.

### Memory Trick
**"Per-Cent" = "Per 100"** — just scale the part up to a 100-point scale. For profit/loss, always subtract the smaller from the bigger, then divide by what you started with (the Cost Price).

### 1-Sentence Summary
Tests your ability to express a part as a fraction of the whole and calculate gains or losses relative to the original amount.

### 30-Second Example
**Q:** 25% of 80?
**A:** 20 — (25 ÷ 100) × 80 = 0.25 × 80 = 20
