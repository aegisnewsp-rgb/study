## Synonyms — Quick Reference

### Key Pattern/Rule
Synonyms are words that share the same or very similar meanings — look for the word that closest matches the given word's sense and intensity.

### Memory Trick
Think of synonyms as "same-team words" — they work in the same situations. Watch out for word intensity: "good" and "excellent" aren't always interchangeable.

### 1-Sentence Summary
GATE tests whether you can identify words with the same meaning, often tricking you with options that are close but not quite right in context.

### Quick Example
Q: Select the synonym of "DILIGENT":
(A) Lazy (B) hardworking (C) careless (D) Slow
A: (B) Hardworking — diligent means showing careful and persistent effort, which is closest to hardworking.
