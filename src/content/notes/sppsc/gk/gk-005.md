## Pakistan's Current Affairs (Recent Developments)

### Political Landscape (Recent)

**Imran Khan and PTI:**
- Imran Khan served as **Prime Minister (2018–2022)**; removed via no-confidence vote in April 2022
- **April 2022 – onwards**: PTI supporters protested extensively; Khan called for early elections
- **May 2023**: Imran Khan arrested in Al-Qadir Trust case; sentenced in multiple cases ( Toshakhana, cipher case)
- **August 2023**: PTI workers clashed with military; party banned from using official symbol (cricket bat)

**Shehbaz Sharif (PML-N) Government:**
- **Shehbaz Sharif** became PM after no-confidence vote (April 2022)
- Coalition government of PML-N, PPP, and allied parties
- **Economy focus**: IMF bailout package discussions; currency stabilization efforts
- **June 2024**: General elections scheduled for February 2024 (held under caretaker setup)

**Caretaker Government (Post-Election):**
- **Anwaar-ul-Haq Kakar** served as Caretaker Prime Minister (August 2023 – March 2024)
- General elections held on **8 February 2024**
- **February 2024 elections**: PTI-backed independent candidates won most NA seats (93+); PML-N and PPP emerged as largest parties post-coalition negotiations

**9th February 2024 — Major Floods:**
- **Climate change** effects: Record monsoon rains caused devastating floods in southern Punjab, Sindh, and Balochistan (2022 floods were more severe historically)
- 2024 flooding displaced millions; significant economic disruption

### Economic Developments

**IMF and Pakistan's Economy:**
- Pakistan has received **multiple IMF bailouts** (most recent: Extended Fund Facility)
- **Current challenges**: high inflation, fiscal deficit, foreign reserves pressure
- **Inflation rate** peaked at 38% (2023); gradually declining in 2024
- **Rupee depreciation**: USD/PKR exchange rate ~280–300 by late 2024

**China-Pakistan Economic Corridor (CPEC):**
- **CPEC Phase II** focuses on: industrial cooperation, agriculture, science & technology
- **ML-1 (Main Line-1) railway project**: major infrastructure under CPEC
- **Gwadar Port**: operational; free economic zone being developed
- **Bahari Haq (Western route)**: connecting Balochistan and Gwadar to national network

**Banking and Energy:**
- **Renewable energy projects**: solar and wind projects under CPEC Phase II
- **RLNG (Regasified LNG)** plants operational for power generation
- **Coal power plants**: Sahiwal, Port Qasim — operational

### Foreign Policy and Relations

**Pakistan-India Relations:**
- Tensions remain over **Kashmir dispute**
- **2023**: Indian restrictions on Kashmiri political leaders continued
- **2024**: No significant diplomatic breakthrough; tensions over terrorism allegations

**Pakistan-China Relations:**
- **All-weather strategic partnership** — China remains Pakistan's closest ally
- **Xi Jinping's 2015 visit**: upgraded relationship to "all-weather strategic cooperative partnership"
- China supported Pakistan on **Uighur issue** in UN; Pakistan supported China on Hong Kong/South China Sea

**Pakistan-Afghanistan (Post-Taliban):**
- **August 2021**: Taliban takeover of Afghanistan
- Pakistan-Taliban border tensions; ** Durand Line** disputes
- **TTP (Tehrik-e-Taliban Pakistan)**: cross-border terrorist attacks increased
- Pakistan closed border crossings multiple times

**Pakistan-UN Relations:**
- Pakistan served on **UN Security Council** (2023–24 term as non-permanent member)
- Active in **UN peacekeeping missions** (among top contributing countries)
- **Climate change**: Pakistan highlighted flood crisis at COP28 (Dubai 2023)

## International Current Affairs

### Key Global Developments

**Ukraine-Russia Conflict (Ongoing since 2022):**
- Conflict continued; **2023–24** saw ongoing military engagement
- Global economic fallout: food price inflation, energy crises
- Pakistan maintained a **neutral stance** while quietly engaging with both parties

**Middle East Developments:**
- **Saudi Arabia–Iran rapprochement** (brokered by China, March 2023): significant diplomatic shift
- **GCC summits** (2023–24): focus on economic integration
- **Palestine issue**: ongoing; UN votes on ceasefire resolutions

**Global Climate Action:**
- **COP28 (Dubai 2023)**: first global stocktake; "transitioning away from fossil fuels"
- **Pakistan Floods 2022**: used as case study for climate vulnerability
- **Loss and Damage Fund**: established at COP27; Pakistan among first beneficiaries

### Regional Organizations

**SAARC (South Asian Association for Regional Cooperation):**
- **Stalled** since 2016 (India-Pakistan tensions after Uri attack)
- Afghanistan admitted 2007; 8 member states
- **Sri Lanka crisis** (2022): SAARC mechanism not activated significantly

**Shanghai Cooperation Organization (SCO):**
- Pakistan is a founding member (2001)
- **2023 SCO Summit** hosted by India
- SCO includes: Russia, China, India, Pakistan, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Tajikistan, Uzbekistan

**Economic Cooperation Organization (ECO):**
- 10 member states: Turkey, Azerbaijan, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Turkmenistan, Uzbekistan, Tajikistan, Iran, Afghanistan, Pakistan
- Focus on trade, energy, transport corridors
