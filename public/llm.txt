# StudyRoadmap — LLM Information File
# Learn more: https://llmstxt.org

## Site Overview
StudyRoadmap is a free, no-signup-required AI study planner for students in India, Pakistan, and Nigeria preparing for competitive exams.

## URL: https://studyroadmap.in

## What We Offer
- Instant, personalized study roadmaps generated client-side using an AI expert system
- Coverage of 21 competitive exams: NEET, JEE Main, JEE Advanced, CUET, UPSC, SSC CGL, CAT, CLAT, NDA, UGC NET, MDCAT, ECAT, NAT-I, LAT, HAT-UG, JAMB, WAEC, NECO, NABTEB, NCEE
- Subject-wise study notes and topic lists for all major subjects
- Roadmap durations from 1 hour to 2 years
- Fully client-side — no data stored, no account needed

## Target Users
- Indian students (NEET, JEE, UPSC, SSC, CAT, CUET, CLAT, NDA, UGC NET)
- Pakistani students (MDCAT, ECAT, NAT-I, LAT, HAT-UG)
- Nigerian students (JAMB, WAEC, NECO, NABTEB, NCEE)
- Parents and educators supporting exam preparation

## Content Type
Educational tool with interactive roadmap generation and reference study notes
