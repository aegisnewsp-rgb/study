## Land and People of Pakistan — Key Facts

### Geography
- **Area**: 796,095 km² (34th largest)
- **Population**: ~240 million
- **Borders**: India (East), Afghanistan (NW), Iran (West), Arabian Sea (South), China (NE)

### Physical Features
- **K2**: 8,611m, 2nd highest in world (Karakoram)
- **Indus River**: 3,180 km through Pakistan
- **Deserts**: Thar (Sindh), Cholistan (Punjab)
- **Coastline**: 1,046 km (Arabian Sea)

### Rivers
- **Indus**: Main river, flows from Tibet to Arabian Sea
- **Tributaries**: Jhelum, Chenab, Ravi, Beas, Sutlej (Punjab rivers)
