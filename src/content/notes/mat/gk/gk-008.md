#### Recent Acts and Amendments

**Major Legislation (2022–2024):**

1. **Digital Personal Data Protection Act (2023):** India's first data protection law; governs how companies collect and process personal data; establishes Data Protection Board of India

2. **Jan Vishwas Act (2023):** Amends 42 Central Acts to decriminalise minor offences and promote ease of doing business

3. **Banking Laws (Amendment) Act (2024):** Allows RBI to supersede banks; increases RBI's supervisory powers

4. **Bhartiya Nyaya Sanhita (2023):** Replaced Indian Penal Code (IPC, 1860); new definitions; community service for petty offences introduced

5. **Bharatiya Nagarik Suraksha Sanhita (2023):** Replaced Criminal Procedure Code (CrPC, 1973); provisions for recording confession at police station; medico-legal care

6. **Bhartiya Sakshya Adhiniyam (2023):** Replaced Indian Evidence Act (1872); electronic evidence provisions updated

7. ** Forest (Conservation) Amendment Act (2023):** Exempts certain forest areas from forest conservation; controversial; states raise concerns

8. **Women Reservation Act (2023):** 33% reservation for women in Lok Sabha and state legislative assemblies; to be implemented after delimitation

#### Constitutional and Legal Updates

**Important Supreme Court Judgments (Recent):**

1. **Same-Sex Marriage (2023):** Supreme Court declined to legalise same-sex marriage under Special Marriage Act; 5-judge bench could not reach consensus; privacy and dignity rights affirmed but legislative action needed

2. **Abortion Rights (2022):** SC upheld women's autonomy and right to abortion (Medical Termination of Pregnancy Act); extended to 24 weeks for special cases; granted dignity to unmarried women

3. **EVM-VVPAT (2024):** SC ruled in favour of EVMs; declined 100% verification; 5% VVPAT count remains

4. **Article 370 (2019):** Supreme Court upheld the Presidential Order (August 2019) that revoked Article 370; Jammu & Kashmir reorganised into two UTs; landmark but controversial

5. **CAG Auditing Powers (2023):** Supreme Court ruled CAG's auditing powers extend to corporations receiving government financial assistance; significant for corporate governance

**Lok Sabha and Rajya Sabha Updates:**

- Parliament's Digital India initiative: e-Sansad, e-Vidhan (digitisation of legislative bodies)
