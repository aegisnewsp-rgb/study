Choosing the right JUPEB subject combination is one of the most consequential academic decisions you'll make. This guide provides strategic frameworks for selecting combinations that maximize your university admission chances and career prospects.

## Strategic Framework for Subject Selection

### Step 1: Identify Your Career Goal
- **Medicine/Dentistry/Pharmacy**: Physics + Chemistry + Biology (PCM or PCB)
- **Engineering**: Physics + Mathematics + Chemistry (PMC or PMF)
- **Law**: Literature + Government + any Arts/Commercial subject
- **Business/Management**: Economics + Accounting + Commerce (or Economics + Government + any)
- **Social Sciences**: Government + Economics + any relevant subject

### Step 2: Assess Your Strengths
- Are you stronger in Sciences or Commercial/Arts subjects?
- Which subjects did you perform best in at O-Level?
- Which subjects do you enjoy studying?

### Step 3: Research University Requirements
Different universities have different requirements even for the same programme:
- **Federal Universities**: Usually have stricter and more uniform requirements
- **State Universities**: May have state-focused quotas and varying requirements
- **Private Universities**: Often more flexible but more expensive

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### Mistake 1: Choosing Based on Peer Pressure
Many students choose combinations their friends are taking without considering their own strengths and goals.

### Mistake 2: Ignoring University Requirements
Some combinations that are valid for one university may not be accepted by another. Always verify requirements at your specific target university.

### Mistake 3: Prioritizing "Easier" Combinations
Some combinations appear easier but may limit your options. A challenging combination (like PCM for Engineering) opens more doors than an easier one that limits your choices.

### Mistake 4: Not Having a Backup Plan
If your first-choice combination doesn't get you admitted, what is your alternative? Consider flexible combinations that allow multiple pathways.

## Most Versatile Combinations

### For Science Students
**Physics + Chemistry + Biology** (PCB):
- Opens: Medicine, Pharmacy, Dentistry, Veterinary Medicine, Biological Sciences, Nursing, Medical Laboratory Science
- Also accepts: Engineering (some schools), Agricultural Sciences

**Physics + Chemistry + Mathematics** (PCM):
- Opens: Engineering, Physical Sciences, Technology, Pharmacy
- Also accepts: Medicine (some schools), Computer Science

### For Commercial Students
**Economics + Accounting + Commerce** (EAC):
- Opens: Accounting, Business Administration, Banking & Finance, Marketing, Insurance
- Also accepts: Economics, Political Science (some schools), Law (with Literature as fourth)

## University Admission Timeline

1. **January-March**: Register for JUPEB programme
2. **May-June**: Write JUPEB examinations
3. **August**: JUPEB results released
4. **August-September**: University PUTME/DE screening begins
5. **September-December**: University admission lists released
6. **January**: University session begins

⚡ **Final JUPEB Tip**: Always have conversations with your school's JUPEB coordinator, current university students in your target programme, and academic counselors before finalizing your combination. University requirements change — what was true last year may not hold this year. Your combination is your academic foundation: choose wisely, then commit fully.
