## Number System & Simplification — Quick Reference

### Key Rule / Formula
LCM × HCF = Product of two numbers. For simplification, always apply BODMAS — Brackets → Orders → Division → Multiplication → Addition → Subtraction.

### Memory Trick
**BODMAS** = "Big Owls Don\'t Mind Adding Subtracting" — brackets first, left to right for same-precedence operations.

### 1-Sentence Summary
SSC tests your ability to find HCF/LCM, handle remainders, simplify expressions, and apply divisibility rules quickly.

### Quick Example
Q: Find the HCF of 48 and 180.
A: **12** — Prime factor method: 48 = 2⁴ × 3, 180 = 2² × 3² × 5. Common factors: 2² × 3 = 12.
