## Percentage — Quick Reference

### Key Formula/Rule
Percent means "per hundred" — 25% = 25/100 = 0.25

### Quick Memory Trick
"Per-Cent" = Per-100. Just divide the percentage by 100 to convert to decimal, or multiply decimal by 100 to get percent.

### 1-Sentence Summary
To find X% of Y, multiply Y by X/100 — that's all percentage ever is.

### Quick Example
Q: What is 20% of 150?
A: 150 × 20/100 = 30

### Must Remember
- X% of Y = Y% of X (20% of 50 = 50% of 20 = 10)
- To increase by X%: multiply by (100+X)/100
- To decrease by X%: multiply by (100-X)/100
- Percentage change = (New - Old)/Old × 100
