## Site Overview
StudyRoadmap is a free, no-signup-required AI study planner for students in India, Pakistan, and Nigeria preparing for competitive exams.

## URL: https://studyroadmap.in

## Date
2026-04-04

## What We Offer
- Instant, personalized study roadmaps generated client-side using an AI expert system
- Coverage of 125+ competitive exams across India, Pakistan, Nigeria, and globally — including NEET, JEE Main, JEE Advanced, CUET, UPSC, SSC CGL, CAT, CLAT, NDA, UGC NET (India), MDCAT, ECAT, NAT-I, LAT, HAT-UG (Pakistan), JAMB, WAEC, NECO, NABTEB, NCEE (Nigeria), and many more
- Subject-wise study notes with tiered content (Quick/Standard/Deep) for NEET, JEE, UPSC, SSC, and other major exams
- Roadmap durations from 1 hour to 2 years
- Fully client-side — no data stored, no account needed

## Target Users
- Indian students (NEET, JEE, UPSC, SSC, CAT, CUET, CLAT, NDA, UGC NET, GRE, and 40+ more)
- Pakistani students (MDCAT, ECAT, NAT-I, LAT, HAT-UG, and more)
- Nigerian students (JAMB, WAEC, NECO, NABTEB, NCEE, and more)
- Students from Bangladesh, Sri Lanka, Nepal, UAE, Saudi Arabia, South Africa, Kenya, and other countries
- Parents and educators supporting exam preparation

## Content Type
Educational tool with interactive roadmap generation and reference study notes. All roadmaps are generated dynamically on the client side based on official exam syllabus data. Study notes are human-authored with tiered depth levels.

## Site Architecture
- Homepage: https://studyroadmap.in/ — FAQ, HowItWorks, Latest Exam Updates (sourced from news.json)
- Exams Directory: https://studyroadmap.in/exams/ — ItemList of all 123 competitive exams
- Individual Exam Pages: https://studyroadmap.in/exams/{examId}/ — exam details, subject cards, HowTo, FAQPage
  Examples: /exams/neet/, /exams/jeemain/, /exams/upsc/, /exams/jamb/, /exams/mdcat/
- Study Notes: https://studyroadmap.in/notes/{examId}/{subjectId}/ — topic listings
- Topic Pages: https://studyroadmap.in/notes/{examId}/{subjectId}/{topicId}/ — detailed topic content
- Roadmap Generator: https://studyroadmap.in/roadmap — interactive AI study plan builder
- About: https://studyroadmap.in/about — editorial team, methodology, reviews
