## Antonyms — Quick Reference

### Key Pattern/Rule
Antonyms are words with directly opposite meanings — spot the negative prefix (un-, in-, dis-, mal-, anti-) or the opposite word entirely.

### Memory Trick
Flip the word to its opposite by adding a negative prefix: "un-" (unhappy), "in-" (invisible), "dis-" (disagree), "anti-" (anti-war), "mal-" (malfunction), "mis-" (misunderstand).

### 1-Sentence Summary
GATE tests your ability to identify the word that means the opposite of the given word, often using academic or infrastructure vocabulary.

### Quick Example
Q: Select the antonym of "BENEVOLENT":
(A) Kind (B) Malevolent (C) Generous (D) Caring
A: (B) Malevolent — benevolent means kind, so malevolent (cruel) is the opposite.
