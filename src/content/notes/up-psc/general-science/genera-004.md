**Indian Economy & Union Budget** — Comprehensive UPPSC PCS Notes
Full coverage: Indian Economy in General Awareness for UPPSC PCS preparation

**Detailed Topics:**
1. **National Income & GDP**
   - Methods of calculating GDP (Income, Expenditure, Value-Added)
   - Limitations of GDP; Human Development Index (HDI) as a supplement
   - Per capita income, PPP (Purchasing Power Parity)

2. **Planning & Development**
   - NITI Aayog: structure, functions, differences from Planning Commission
   - Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) — India's rank and targets
   - Aspirational Districts Programme

3. **Money, Banking & RBI**
   - Functions of RBI: issuing currency, government banker, regulator
   - Monetary Policy: Repo, Reverse Repo, MSF, CRR, SLR
   - Inflation: Types (Demand-Pull, Cost-Push), effects, remedies
   - Financial Inclusion: Jan Dhan, Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT)

4. **Union Budget**
   - Budget documents: Annual Financial Statement, Demands for Grants, Finance Bill
   - Types of receipts: Revenue vs Capital; Tax vs Non-tax revenue
   - Types of expenditure: Plan vs Non-Plan (discontinued from 2017), Revenue vs Capital
   - Fiscal Responsibility and Budget Management (FRBM) Act
   - Deficits: Fiscal, Revenue, Primary — formula and significance

5. **Taxation & GST**
   - Direct Taxes: Income Tax, Corporate Tax
   - Indirect Taxes: GST — subsuming VAT, excise, service tax
   - GST Council: composition and role

6. **Key Economic Schemes & Institutions**
   - MGNREGA: guarantee of 100 days employment, wages
   - PM-KISAN: income support to farmers
   - Ayushman Bharat: health insurance coverage
   - Startup India, Stand-Up India, Mudra Yojana

7. **External Sector**
   - Balance of Payments: Current Account, Capital Account
   - Foreign Exchange: Rupee depreciation/ appreciation, RBI intervention
   - FEMA vs FERA (historical context)

Practice: Attempt previous year UPPSC PCS questions on Indian Economy. Focus on UP-specific economic data (industrial corridors, agricultural statistics) where available.
