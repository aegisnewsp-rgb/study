## Sentence Improvement — Quick Reference

### Key Pattern/Rule
Pick the clearest, most concise, grammatically correct version — often the shortest one that preserves meaning.

### Memory Trick
"C3LS" — Clarity, Conciseness, Correctness, Logic, Style. If two options say the same thing, choose the shorter one.

### 1-Sentence Summary
You choose which of four rewrites best improves the original sentence in clarity, concision, grammar, and style without changing the intended meaning.

### Quick Example
Q: "The reason is because she was late."
A: (D) **The reason is that she was late** — "Because" directly after "reason" is redundant; use "that" instead.
