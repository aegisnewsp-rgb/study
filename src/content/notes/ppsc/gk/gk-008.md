# Pakistan's Cultural Heritage

### 🟢 Lite — Quick Review (1h–1d)
> Rapid summary for last-minute revision before your PPSC exam.

## Pakistan's Cultural Heritage — Key Facts

### UNESCO World Heritage Sites
1. **Mohenjo-daro** (Sindh): Indus Valley Civilization
2. **Taxila** (Punjab): Ancient Buddhist city
3. **Takht-i-Bahi** (KP): Buddhist monastery
4. **Shalimar Gardens** (Lahore): Mughal gardens
5. **Rohtas Fort** (Punjab): Mughal fort
6. **Makli Necropolis** (Sindh): Largest grave site
7. **Shah Jahan Mosque** (Thatta, Sindh): Beautiful mosque

### National Symbols
- **Flag**: Green with white crescent and star
- **National Animal**: Markhor
- **National Flower**: Jasmine
- **Capital**: Islamabad

### Cultural Diversity
- Punjabis, Pashtuns, Sindhis, Baloch, Muhajirs
- Multiple languages, traditions, crafts
