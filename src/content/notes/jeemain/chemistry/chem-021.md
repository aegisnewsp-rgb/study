### Lite Quick Review (1h-1d)

**Haloalkanes Key Facts for JEE Main**
- Haloalkanes are aliphatic compounds where one or more hydrogen atoms are replaced by halogen (F, Cl, Br, I).
- Classification: based on halogen (fluoro, chloro, bromo, iodo) and on carbon type (primary, secondary, tertiary).
- Physical properties: boiling point increases with atomic size and number of halogen atoms; density > water for many chloro/bromo/iodo.
