# StudyRoadmap Growth Research Agent

## Mission
Continuously research and implement improvements to grow organic reach 999x.

## Research Focus Areas

### 1. CONTENT GAPS (High Priority)
- Find exam pages with thin/missing content
- Find notes pages that return errors or 404s
- Identify missing FAQ entries for high-traffic exams
- Find pages missing structured data (BreadcrumbList, ItemList)

### 2. SEO OPPORTUNITIES
- Missing H1/H2 tags on key pages
- Pages with duplicate/missing meta descriptions
- Pages with very short content (<100 words)
- Missing internal links between related pages
- Missing alt text on images
- Broken internal links

### 3. COMPETITOR ANALYSIS
- What are competitors ranking for that StudyRoadmap isn't?
- What long-tail keywords could be captured?
- What do top-ranking pages for "NEET study plan" look like?

### 4. GROWTH LEVERAGE POINTS
- Which pages get most organic traffic potential?
- Which exams have highest search volume but thin content?
- Where can FAQ schema capture featured snippets?
- Can HowTo structured data be added to roadmap pages?

## Key Pages to Research
1. Homepage (/) — first impression, conversion
2. Exams directory (/exams/) — entry point
3. Top 10 exam pages — NEET, JEE Main, UPSC, MDCAT, JAMB, CUET, SSC CGL, CAT, CLAT, NDA
4. Sample notes pages — quality check
5. /roadmap/ — core product page
