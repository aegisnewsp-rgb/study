## Number System — Quick Reference

### Key Formula/Rule
**HCF × LCM = Product of the two numbers** (for any pair of integers)

### Memory Trick
Think of HCF as the **biggest common factor** (what overlaps in a factor Venn diagram) and LCM as the **smallest common multiple** (the shared finish line when counting in each number's multiples). Their product always equals the original pair's product — that's your freebie formula!

### 1-Sentence Summary
This topic tests your ability to find common factors and multiples quickly, plus spot divisibility patterns without doing long division.

### 30-Second Example
Q: Find the HCF of 12 and 18.
A: **6** — common factors are {1,2,3,6}; greatest is 6.

Q: What is the remainder when 47 is divided by 5?
A: **2** — 47 = 5 × 9 + 2.
