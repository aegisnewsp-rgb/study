## Analogy — Quick Reference

### Key Pattern/Rule
Analogy = identify the relationship in the first pair, then find the same relationship between the second pair.

### Memory Trick
Analogy questions are about relationships, not words. Say to yourself: "As [word A] is to [word B], so is [word C] to ___?" — then fill in the blank by finding the same connection.

### 1-Sentence Summary
GATE tests your ability to recognize logical relationships between words and apply those relationships to find the missing pair.

### Quick Example
Q: JOURNEY : DESTINATION :: STUDY :
(A) Book (B) Exam (C) Knowledge (D) School
A: (C) Knowledge — journey leads to destination; study leads to knowledge.
