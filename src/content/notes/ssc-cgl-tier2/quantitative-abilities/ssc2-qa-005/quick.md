## Ratio, Proportion & Partnership — Quick Reference

### Key Rule / Formula
Ratio a:b = a/b. If a:b = c:d, then a×d = b×c (cross-multiplication). Partnership profit/loss divided in ratio of capital × time invested.

### Memory Trick
**"Capital × Time = Share"** — for partnerships, both money AND duration matter. If A invests for 6 months and B for 4 months, their effective capital contribution = Capital × Months.

### 1-Sentence Summary
SSC tests whether you can divide quantities in given ratios and distribute partnership profits correctly based on each partner's time-weighted capital contribution.

### Quick Example
Q: Divide 144 in the ratio 3:4:5.
A: Sum = 12 parts. 144/12 = 12. Parts are 36, 48, 60.
